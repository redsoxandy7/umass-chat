About Us

We are a group of students who attend Umass Boston. Our goal is to create a chat Application that students can use with other students at the University of Massachusetts Boston. We want to give students the opportunity to be able to do any type of work that involves collabration to instantly login with there Umass Boston credentials and be able to chat and work together, without the hassle of using a 3rd party application.
